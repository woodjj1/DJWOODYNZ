As a current student studying a bachelor's degree in I.T. at Otago Polytechnic, I am constantly working on learning new aspects of the industry.  

From new frameworks to new computer languages,  I.T. does offer a lot of challenges.  

I try my best in all aspects of life and believe in giving everything a go. 

Being awarded Otago Polytechnic's most promising first year in 2016,  and best project for the Dunedin Refugee Online Presence in 2017 was a great honor.

I have always enjoyed music but started djing well over 15 years.  I dj all types of events from artist tours, nightclubs, weddings and other functions.

 A highlight in my dj career was being the official opening dj act for the band Six60 and Tinnie Tempah in 2017 at Forsyth Barr Stadium to 5000 people.
